Spain

During the summer of 2022, I participated in a psychology-focused study abroad program based in Madrid, Spain. We frequently took trips outside the city to explore the rest of the country, and the shots displayed below are a product of my awe for the scenery and the architectural fusion that the country has to offer. Due to my lack of access to a “real camera,” I had no choice but to use my iPhone 11’s less-than-optimal lens. Nevertheless, I still believe they encapsulate my memories of the places I visited very well.
