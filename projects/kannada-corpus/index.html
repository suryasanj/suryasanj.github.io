Surya Sanjay

This is part of a series on Canarese (Kannada) literature. The Vikramārjunavijayam was written by the author Pampa in the 10th century. Below is the text, with a poetic translation and links to dictionary entries in Kittel’s Kannada dictionary.

Pampakavi Viracitam
 Vikramārjunavijayam

Prathamāśvāsam

Unlike Narayana, who obtained Lakshmi without any effort, who obtained the earth from the king of Bali, and who wore the ordinary crown of Pushpapatta on his head, may the supreme Narayana, who has defeated the enemy kings without seeking Jayalakshmi by churning the ocean of enemy armies, and who has obtained the earth, the crown of Suppatha, befitting his merit, grant us a wealth of happiness. śrīyan arāti-‌ sādhana-payōnidhiyoḷ paḍedum dharitriyan
jīyₐ ene bēḍi-koḷḷade virōdhi narēndraran otti-koṇḍum ā—
tmīya-supuṣpa-vr̥ṣṭiyan oḍambaḍe tāḷdiyum intₐ udātta-nā—
rāyaṇan āda dēvan emagₑ īgₑ arikēsari saukhya kōṭiyan
