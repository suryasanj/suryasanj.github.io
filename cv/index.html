вҢ– College of Engineering, University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, MI
оҖҒ My work at the Cardio Biomechanics Lab, advised by Dr. David Nordsletten, has mostly focused on using computational fluid dynamics (CFD) simulations to model the effects of cardiac procedures on hemodynamics. During my most recent project, which looked into the effects of aortic root enlargement (ARE) on hemostasis, I got acquainted with various biomechanics and modeling software, such as 3D Slicer, ParaView, and CHeart вҖ” a finite-element code to solve multiphysics problems in cardiac physiology.

вҢ– University of Michigan Medical School, Ann Arbor, MI

Most of my prior research experience had been computational, and, as such, the Brooks Lab gave me an opportunity to gain exposure to various wet-lab techniques, including immunocytochemistry (ICC), RNA extraction, cell plating, and using a Lionheart microscope for imaging.
