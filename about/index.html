Who are you?

My name is Surya Sanjay, and I accept three pronunciations of my name: an Indianized [ˈsuːɾja(ː) ˈsʌnd͡ʒɛɪ̯], an Americanized [ˈsɝjə ˈsɑnd͡ʒej], and a hybrid [ˈsuːɹjə ˈsənd͡ʒej]. If you’re not a linguist and these symbols look unfamiliar to you, these phonetic notations are transcribed using the ipa (the International Phonetic Alphabet, not India Pale Ale).
