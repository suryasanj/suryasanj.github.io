About

As of Feb 2023, I’m a sophomore at the University of Michigan studying Biopsychology, Cognition, & Neuroscience and pursuing a career in medicine.

What are you working on?

  1. My main goal is to become a physician; right now, I’m seriously considering cardiothoracic surgery as a specialty.
  2. Outside of school, my largest commitment is research. I’m part of two computational biology labs, and I do linguistics research on the side. See more in my CV.
  3. I’m part of two campus organizations — Michigan Synthetic Biology Team and the Phi Delta Epsilon Pre-Medical Fraternity.
  4. In my free time, I like to volunteer at a nearby hospital and shadow surgeries in the OR.

What are your hobbies?

  1. ✈ Ever since I was a kid, I’ve been in love with planes, and I always used to enjoy planespotting at the airport at playing all varieties of flight simulators. I hope to get my pilot’s license at some point in the future.
  2. ⛹︎ Admittedly, I’m a bit new to it, but I try to weightlift at least three times a week.
  3. ⛾︎ Although I’m far from the classic college-student coffee addiction, I love to explore cafés wherever I am. At Michigan, my favorite is probably M-36.
  4. ♫︎ For my 16ᵗʰ birthday, my friends gifted me a record player. Ever since, I’ve collected Spirits Having Flown by the Bee Gees, War and Leisure by Miguel, Heartbeat City by the Cars, and American Heartbreak by Zach Bryan.
